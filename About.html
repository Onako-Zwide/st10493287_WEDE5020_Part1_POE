Our Background

FreshMart ORGANICS was founded in 2017 by visionary entrepreneurs Onako Zwide and Alizwa Tshantshi, who shared a deep passion for health, sustainability, and community empowerment. The journey began on a modest farm nestled in the heart of Makhanda, where the founders cultivated organic produce using eco-conscious methods and a commitment to chemical-free farming.

Recognizing the growing demand for wholesome, natural foods, they launched a small stall at the local farmer’s market. Their dedication to quality and transparency quickly earned them a loyal following. Customers appreciated not only the freshness of the products but also the values behind them—supporting local agriculture, reducing environmental impact, and promoting wellness.

As word spread, FreshMart ORGANICS evolved from a humble stall into a trusted retail brand. Today, it offers a wide range of organic fruits, vegetables, dairy alternatives, skincare items, and eco-friendly household products. The founders’ original mission remains at the heart of the business: to ensure that every South African has access to healthy, organic foods and sustainable products that nourish both body and planet.

FreshMart ORGANICS continues to grow, driven by a commitment to ethical sourcing, community partnerships, and innovation in organic retail. From farm to shelf, every product tells a story of care, integrity, and a greener future.

Mission

To provide fresh, affordable organic products and promote healthy living through sustainable practices and online shopping.

Vision

To become South Africa’s favorite organic and eco-friendly retailer, supporting farmers and protecting the environment.

Goals

  • Support Local Farmers
